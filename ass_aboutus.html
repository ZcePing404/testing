The Story of Koala Tea

The beautiful heritage of tea faces the danger of being forgotten every day, and so one day, a team of tea lovers decided to take on the mission of resuscitating and reintroducing the Malaysia tea culture to the world — with a modern twist. And thus, Koala Tea was born. Koala Tea is a fresh milk tea shop whose sole mission was to reintroduce and bring back the Malaysia culture of tea to the world and has done so in a most excellent way.

Our shop opened in 2018 and started as a small shop through years and years of trying out the best recipe for a milk tea,until now we are the best in Malaysia!

Koala insists on using original premium tea leaves from Koala Organic Tea Farm located at 1,588 metres above sea level in Cameron Highlands, which serves as the optimal atmosphere for the growth of tea plants.

Using only imported fresh New Zealand milk to pair with our premium tea as New Zealand produces some of the world's best quality milk.

Each and every local daily supplied fresh fruits are carefully picked to assure their freshness to be coupled with our premium tea.
